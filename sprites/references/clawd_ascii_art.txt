## Notes
- clawd_body is the orange-salmon color used for the crab outline/legs
- The body area uses clawd_body as fg on clawd_background (black) bg
  creating filled black squares with orange block borders
- The Apple Terminal version uses inverse (bg:clawd_body) for a 
  rounder look without the ▛▜▝▘ quarter-block characters
- Official name: "Clawd" (Claude + Claw pun, 🦀)
